BrickBuddy

AI-Powered LEGO Robot Building Assistant for Children (6-8) | Yusong HUANG, Jiayi HUANG | CMAA5043 Milestone Check-in

Project Goal

  • Multimodal AI assistant guiding kids through LEGO robot building
  • Voice + camera + template input for natural child expression
  • Personalized step-by-step instructions with emotional support
  • Integrated STEAM education throughout the building process

3-Stage Interaction Flow

  • Stage 1 - Imagine: Child describes/shows robot idea via voice, camera, or templates
  • Stage 2 - Build: AI generates adaptive instructions + real-time chat support
  • Stage 3 - Learn: STEAM Q&A summarizes science, tech, engineering, art, math concepts

STEAM Integration

Science Technology Engineering Art Math

Interface Design (5 Screens)

  • Splash: Welcome mascot + "Let's Build!" CTA
  • Imagine: Voice/camera/template multimodal input
  • Build: Step-by-step instructions + AI chat sidebar
  • Learn: STEAM Q&A cards with fun facts
  • Celebrate: Confetti + progress stats + learning summary
