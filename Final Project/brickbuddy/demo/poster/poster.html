BrickBuddy
CMAA5043 · Final Project · Spring 2026
Meet BrickBuddy

Show the pile.
Pick a build. Change anything.

An AI building companion for children aged 6–8. Scan your bricks, pick a robot, then change it by voice or tap — and watch the instruction manual rewrite itself in real time.

The booklet

One-way paper.

  • Can't see what bricks the kid actually has
  • Can't answer "why?" or adapt mid-build
  • Can't recolor when they want something different
  • Only shows one "correct" model
→
BrickBuddy

A living manual.

  • Scans the pile, recommends a build
  • "Make it blue" — 3D and manual rewrite themselves
  • Tap a brick to recolor only that one
  • Teaches STEAM through every step
Every session follows one arc · five screens, zero menus

Six design decisions — the moat is the stack
📖
Living manual

One mutation engine drives both 3D and printed manual. Voice, tap, structural — same source, never out of sync.

⚡
Local first

Every voice and tap mod is parsed deterministically on-device. Zero LLM stalls, zero offline failure.

🧩
Data bricks

Models are declarative JSON. The same 3D pipeline renders any robot the child imagines tomorrow.

🎯
Region aware

"Make the head purple" works because the parser knows aliases — head = snout = face. Voice scopes to a region; tap scopes to one brick.

🪶
Zero install

A URL, a webcam, a microphone. Works on a Chromebook, iPad, or any laptop. Deploys to Cloudflare's edge.

👶
Six-year UX

Big type, warm palette, always-visible mic, no hidden menus. Every screen is single-finger reachable.
